COURSE & COLLEGE DETAILS

Diploma in Civil Engineering

Diploma in Civil Engineering is a 3 year course for students after completion of Class 10 that teaches students to plan,design, execute and maintain structural works like bridges, buildings, roads and other infrastructural projects.

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering is a twelfth level Diploma course which deals with the field of engineering. Mechanical engineering is a discipline of engineering that applies the principles of physics and materials science for analysis, design, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems.

Diploma in Computer Engineering

Diploma in Computer Engineering is a 3 year course commonly called the CME. Computer Engineering is the study of computational systems, which students are taught about computer technology (hardware and software). Computer science is also the study of processes that interact with data.

Diploma in Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Diploma inElectrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) is a 3 year polytechnic Course that aims in providing knowledge and training on the discipline of both Electrical and Electronics Engineering streams. This includes principles of physics, mathematicswith electricity, electronics and electromagnetism.

Diploma in Electronics & Communication Engineering

Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering Overview. Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering(ECE) is a 3-year course that focuses on understanding electronics networks and devices, electric magnetic fields, computer fundamentals, telecommunications, and control systems.

Diploma in Applied Electronics& Instrumentation Engineering

Applied Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering is an advanced branch of engineering which deals with the application of existing or known scientific knowledge in electronics, instrumentation, measurements and control for any process, practical calibration of instruments, automation of processes etc.

Diploma in Chemical Engineering

Diploma in Chemical Engineering is a three-year long programme. This course teaches the students about chemicals and raw materials and ways of creating new materials out of existing chemicals.
